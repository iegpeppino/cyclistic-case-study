Introduction

Cyclistic, the bike sharing company would like us to find how annual members and casual riders use their service differently. In base of that premise, Iâll analyze their latest yearly dataset to gain insights that would help us convince casual riders into becoming annual members. Step 5: Exporting the results

These summarized statistics were exported for further analysis in a spreadsheet software. The following tables and visualizations were created, that would let us arrive our conclusions.

This table contains data about the number of rides and average ride time per weekday for casual riders and members :

library(DT)
## Warning: package 'DT' was built under R version 4.2.1
datatable(num_rides_count, rownames= FALSE, filter= "top", options= list(pageLength= 7, scrollX= T))


Line graphs were created based on this table, clearly letting us see the changes on number of rides and average ride time through the week :

The number of rides from casual riders significantly drops on weekdays, with the highest number of rides taking place on Sundays (597000) and the lowest on Tuesdays (300841), nearly halving their highest number. For members, the number of rides increment ever so slightly from weekends to weekdays. Their highest number of rides is on Tuesdays (557319),and their lowest on Saturdays (435625). Casual riders high increment in riding for the weekends would complement the decrements membersâ ridership suffers on these days.


Casual riders have overall higher ride duration than members, but itâs decrease through the week is more pronounced. A hypothesis would be, that casual riders, use the service for leisure activities such as sight-seen, thus taking more time in their rides. In the other hand, members would be using the service to commute to work, school or to do chores, having less distractions and a more direct route, hence the shorter ride times. Further data collection and analysis would be needed to confirm this hypothesis, such as a survey asking for what purpose clients use the service, and if they are annual members or not.
